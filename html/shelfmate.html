    Shelfmate

    With Shelfmate, We introduces a new and modern take on shelving units, as well as a new style in wall decoration.

    A system of variously sized shelving units to dress up your wall. Use the shelves alone or combined together: the composition possibilities are endless. The shelves are handcrafted out of high-quality reclaimed teak wood, supported by an iron frame, which contributes to a more modern look that suits many different types of interiors.
